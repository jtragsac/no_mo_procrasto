No Mo' Procrasto | |__| | Time to stay on task'o

Purpose

In stressful situations people tend to procrastinate. Even the most purest and innocent of all . . . probably drift into an abyss.

With this window open you will be automatically reminded not to. The start button will prompt you for a time. That time will determine how long you will not be reminded. Once you set the time be free to work on whatever!
