No Mo' Procrasto | Please Stay on Task'O

Purpose

In stressful situations people tend to procrastinate. Even the most purest and innocent of all probably drift into the procrastination abyss. To the guy in front of you, the guy behind you, the guy next to you, the guy flying above you, the guy inside you, a guy walking, a guy playing league of legends, or the guy eating tacos with red jamaica tea, we all sometimes get off task. We all laugh when we joke about putting aside the work. But in the end we know it's painful. So what can we do to stop this dilema! Well this app won't stop it, but it will try to help.

Instructions

With this window open you will be automatically reminded not to. The start button will prompt you for a time and websites you are avoiding. First type the website you are avoiding, then type in the time in terms of minutes. That time will determine how long you will not be reminded and a red banner reminding you to stay on task will replace this text.

Once you set the time be free to work on whatever! If you want, too, there are study playlists linked to youtube, soundcloud, and spotify
